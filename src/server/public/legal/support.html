Common questions

Why are some prices different from TCGplayer.com?
We refresh market data daily. Prices on the app may lag the live site by up to 24 hours.

How do I delete my account?
Open the Profile tab and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes your account and all data.

Why don't I see all One Piece cards?
We track cards with a market price above $5 to keep the dataset focused on cards you might actually buy or trade.

The app crashed. What should I do?
Crashes are reported automatically (anonymized) so we can fix them. If a crash keeps happening, email us with what you were doing when it happened.
